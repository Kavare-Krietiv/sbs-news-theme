ABOUT US

We are Shungwaya Broadcasting Services abreviated to “SBS” which is a Regional Media House licensed to serve throughout the Coast Region and began broadcasting on Frequency 94.4 FM Kilifi on the 27th August 2018 from its transmitters in Mambrui and Jaribuni covering all the six coastal counties: Kilifi, Mombasa, Kwale, Taita - Taveta, Lamu and Tana River.

Our mission has remained the same for almost 3 years, acting in the public interest, serving all audiences through

the provision of impartial, high-quality and distinctive output and services which inform, educate and entertain. The public purposes outline the values we hold when striving to achieve our mission to inform, educate and entertain and they provide a clear framework through which we are held to account.

  • To provide impartial news and information to help people to understand and engage with the world around them.
  • To provide impartial news and information to help people to understand and engage with the world around them.
  • To support learning for people of all ages.
  • To show the most creative, highest quality and distinctive output and services.
  • To reflect, represent and serve the diverse communities of this nation and, in doing so, support the creative economy across the the nation and beyond.
  • To reflect kenya, its culture and values to the world.

Message from CEO

Nowhere is this more obvious than in the quality of our output. Audiences in their thousands have been gripped by hourly news, live and recorded talk shows, hit music across all genres, from features, weather updates to analysis such as environmental, health, socio- economic education and dramatic change that are affecting the community on daily basis. Meanwhile, our SBS news teams have kept audiences up to date on every twist and turn of our Nation’s development, based on a tireless commitment to impartiality, fact- checking, and in-depth analysis.

As CEO, I am pleased that this profile will put us on the map, get our radio known to a greater audience and demonstrates how the audiences may benefit from SBS. This is why since our inception our main goal has been reinventing public service broadcasting for a new generation responding, in particular, to the challenge presented by well-resourced competitors who are changing the way audiences consume audio. This means modernising our services for the digital age – a key focus for us this year.

f you should ever traverse the airwaves moving from frequency to frequency, our guarantee is that you will not find a fresher, edgier sound than SBS. SBS Programmes have their finger on the pulse of the Coast; our shows are informative, fun and definitely set the trend. With energetic, witty and lively presenters, we are often imitated, but never duplicated. In this era of high competition in the radio market, stations continue to try and replicate the programming, style and format of SBS, but with little success. We remain relevant, entertaining, informative and interactive all day.

Our Early Morning show ‘Anza siku na Mungu’ (4am- 6am) handles Wake up calls, online church, prayers, Worship, promos and activations and a touch of Gospel Music.

Our Breakfast show ‘Kilingeni’ (6am- 10am) handles issues on Wake up calls, Politics, Issues affecting society, Business and traffic, Newspaper headlines, Weather, Promotions and activations. News and a touch of Lingala Music. It’s witty, fast-paced and full of laughs.

If you love a Mixture of tunes!, want to know about the latest fashion and style, gadgets, hangouts cool drinks, Activations and promos, Entertainment gossip, Spoken words, Beauty tips, hot outfits and News, our mid-morning ‘Planet Mziki’ show (10am – 1pm) will take care of you – the young and upcoming socialite.

Right after lunch SBS offers you a two hours Salamu Zako show Active every weekday (1pm-3pm) to help you unwind as you sit back and listen to greetings, activation and promos and news with a mixture of various tunes .

The afternoon airwaves on SBS are ruled by ‘The SBS Reggae Connect’ (3pm-4pm) a fun-filled, raw, intelligent and sometimes cocky but always entertaining show, with the most unique style of music formatting each day – from the latest classic and New Reagge hits.

‘SBS Evening Drive I’ comes next (4pm-5:30pm) this show has attitude and energy, and gives you everything from Patashika viwanjani, Sports news, Transfer rumors, Sports results, Team news, Activations and promos with a touch of Lingala and Rhumba.

After the high-octane energy of ‘SBS Evening Drive I’ (5:30pm-6pm) , slow it down with ‘SBS Evening Drive II’ – our slow seductive way of winding down your evening with Leo Bungeni, Views, Politics, Development issues, Opinions, Rumors Actualities, Activations and promos with atouch of Rhumba.

‘SBS Evening Drive III’ comes next (6pm-8pm) this show gives you everything from Afya yetu, Health issues, Interviews, Analysis, Activations and promos Activations and promos with a touch of Lingala and Rhumba.

For all you night owls and other audience in different time zones, ‘SBS Rhumba Night’ is definitely your show, with Entertainment, News, Analysis, Opinions, Business, Activations and promos as you prepare to call it a night.

As your Official weekend Station, we give you an equally amazing line up of SBS Kilingeni, Watoto wa SBS, Bustani ya Taarab, Mirindimo ya Bango, Patashika Viwanjani, Kilimo na Biashara and SBS Rhumba Nite.

Sunday mornings are kicked off with an inspirational Baraka Tele followed by Ijue Lugha, Liwazo Mchana, Wanawake na Ugatuzi, and Reggae Club. To wind down the weekend catch up with the only and Biggest Gospel show ‘Baraka Tele Jioni’.

The world has become a global village, and SBS has found a way to connect all listeners worldwide through the SBS Digital Media. This interactive media service connects the SBS family to friends and fans all over the world by use of its website and Mobile App.

The SBS Sales Team is client-driven, working as consultant rather than just regular sales representatives or account managers. Want to know more about our competitive rates, our account managers will be your perfect guides. Marketing in SBS is the nerve centre of the organization. SBS Marketing goes beyond the call of duty to link the client, the listener and the station. SBS Marketing keeps our brand strong and recognizable. As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility, we intend to initiate projects that uphold humanity and the rights of the less fortunate. We also intend to sponsor organizations that do so.

SBS Creative, a department set up recently provides an integrated service available to all advertisers, advertising agencies and promotions companies alike, forming a strategic arm of SBS’s commercial production and promotions division.

The Department will consistently manage to keep up with high quality commercials and the best on-air promotions for the biggest brands in Kenya. The department intends to be innovative, competent and able to give your brand that extra edge needed to make an impact in the market.

David C. Ngumbao,
CEO, SBS.
